    ABOUT MTV SHUGA NAIJA 5 - STAYING ALIVE

    ABOUT MTV SHUGA NAIJA 5 - STAYING ALIVE MTV Shuga is a multi-award-winning TV drama series that launched in Kenya in 2009, it integrates health messaging into storylines that reflect the lives and experiences of youth audiences. Since launching, MTV-SAF has gone ahead to film in Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa, Côte d’Ivoire, and India. The drama has touched on numerous plotlines relating to Family planning & contraception, HIV and safe sex. The show is broadcast globally on 164 broadcasters in 73 countries.

    MTV Shuga Naija season5 is on with key messaging focused on COVID-19 and promoting vaccine uptake, HIV/AIDS self-testing and prevention, female empowerment, modern contraception, and gender-based violence among young people.
